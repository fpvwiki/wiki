A generic LiPO [[Battery]] alarm will being emitting loud buzzing sound when voltage gets too low. Could be found online for about $5.

## Advantages

* Cheapest way to tell when the battery is getting low.
* Can pass it easily to a friend.
* Can connect to [[Ground Station]] LiPO battery.

## Disadvantages

* If you flying with a friend who has similar alarm, it could be difficult to tell whose alarm is going off.
